  1. I am lost and I need context. What is "HZ" and what exactly is this website?

  3. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

  4. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

    First, log in to your personal MyHZ Portal. Second, navigate to your personal menu settings, and add the 'Webshop' widget to the menu, save it, then go back to your dashboard and click on the link. If you have ever ordered any item anywhere on the item, then you'll know where to go from here.

  5. How can you book a project space?

    First, log in to your personal MyHZ Portal. Second, navigate to your personal menu settings, and add the 'Self Service Portal' widget to the menu, save it, then go back to your dashboard and click on the link. Click on the 'Reservations' square, click on the 'How do I make a reservation?' square if you require further instructions, otherwise just click on the 'Reserve a meeting or project room' square.

  6. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    For Het Groene Woud:

    Instruction #1: Have some sort of disability or chronic condition.

    Instruction #2: Profit.

    Otherwise:

    Instruction #1: Park at Parkeergarage Kousteensedijk, Middelburg.
