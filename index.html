Why hast thou picked the HZ HBO-ICT programme?

I enjoy stimulating challenges. The ICT-field offers these. It is, to me, just the right blend of research and application. Ever since I was young, I've been able to enter 6+ hour flow states where I work continuously, whether that be something creative, something technical, or studying for school. These flow states assisted me in building this website. As for why I am the way I am, I suppose it's just a combination between genetics and environment that eventually made me the person I am today. At a base level, this is simply what my being is attracted to, deeper than that I cannot go. Even one's degree of self-determination and metacognition is predetermined.

Former experience and proof for my claims.

The first piece of proof is, no doubt, this website. Some features took me hours to implement because my brain enjoys overcomplicating even the most basic problems. I learnt HTML and CSS using W3Schools, MDN as well as FreeCodeCamp. In addition, unrelated to this website, I've build simple applications for forward-projected investment returns as well as math problems in Python, and am currently working through LearnCPP in my free time. One thing I enjoy about C++ is that incentivizes learning about memory allocation, as well as basic computer science to understand how the code you write is translated into machine code, allowing you more clarity on topics like memory managment and optimization, wheras a higher-level programming language like Python doesn't require you to do that.

What I'm hoping to get out of this course

  • Time management. Slow learner and chronic slacker here. I would do well with structure and deadlines.
  • Figuring out what employers want from me. How can I make myself useful?
  • Expose myself to stressors that reveal my strengths and weak points. "Software engineer, know thyself!"
